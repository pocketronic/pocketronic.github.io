Frequently Asked Questions
Is there an iPhone/iPod touch version of Next Thing?
Yes! Next Thing is fully Universal. That means you only need to buy it once to run it on your iPad, your iPhone and your iPod touch.

If you bought Next Thing before it became Universal, you don't need to buy anything else—just make sure you download the latest update from within iTunes.

What do I do if I have a different question?
Email us! We might even add your question to the list.
Does Next Thing sync with my computer?
Next Thing doesn't share tasks with any Mac or Windows software or web service. We may offer this feature in the future.

However, your tasks and settings are fully backed up when you sync your device with iTunes, in case you ever need to restore them.
